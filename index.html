Our story

We've done this together for 20 years, and we built something that scaled.

Before rSportz, Greg built at the edge of what hardware and software could do together. At Geocast, a Kleiner Perkins–backed startup, he helped engineer “the Brick” — a device that pulled data down over unused digital TV spectrum. He then founded GoGoCast, deploying a network of 1,100 screens into convenience stores nationwide, each one controlled wirelessly.

In 2006, Greg started rSportz and brought Todd in at inception to raise capital, acquire the software platform that became its core, and scale the company.

That acquisition secured the lead investor who backed rSportz with more than $25M. Over the next decade-plus, the platform grew to serve over 3 million athletes across 100+ countries before its 2020 exit, with Greg as CTO through 2021.
